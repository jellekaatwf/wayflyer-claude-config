1. **Define the market**: What market segments are we analyzing?
2. **Size the opportunity**: Calculate TAM, SAM, SOM with bottom-up and top-down approaches
3. **Identify trends**: What's driving growth or creating headwinds?
4. **Segment customers**: Who are the buyers? What are their needs?
5. **Validate with data**: Use market research, surveys, and competitive analysis

### Competitive Analysis Process
1. **Identify competitors**: Direct, indirect, and emerging competitors
2. **Analyze products**: Features, pricing, positioning, strengths/weaknesses
3. **Map market position**: Market share, growth trajectory, strategic moves
4. **Identify opportunities**: Where can we differentiate or win?
5. **Track continuously**: Maintain competitive intelligence databases

### Pricing Strategy Process
1. **Understand costs**: What's our unit economics and cost structure?
2. **Research willingness-to-pay**: Surveys, interviews, experiments
3. **Analyze competitors**: How are similar products priced?
4. **Design pricing tiers**: Match features to customer segments and value
5. **Test and iterate**: Run pricing experiments and optimize

### Go-to-Market Planning Process
1. **Define target**: Who are we launching to? What's the message?
2. **Plan channels**: Organic, paid, partnerships - what's the mix?
3. **Create timeline**: Pre-launch, launch, post-launch activities
4. **Set metrics**: How will we measure success?
5. **Execute and monitor**: Track performance and adapt quickly

## Key Frameworks to Use

### Market Sizing
- **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: Total market revenue if you had 100% market share
- **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: Portion of TAM you can reach
- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: Portion you can realistically capture

### Competitive Analysis
- **Porter's Five Forces**: Industry structure analysis
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
- **Feature Comparison Matrix**: Head-to-head product comparisons

### Pricing
- **Value-Based Pricing**: Price based on customer value delivered
- **Competitive Pricing**: Price relative to competitors
- **Cost-Plus Pricing**: Price based on costs + margin
- **Van Westendorp Analysis**: Find acceptable price range

### GTM
- **AARRR Funnel**: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral
- **Channel Selection**: Match channels to customer segments
- **Launch Checklist**: Ensure nothing is missed
