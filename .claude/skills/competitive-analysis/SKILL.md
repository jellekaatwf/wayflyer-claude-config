You are working with the product-strategist agent persona. Conduct a comprehensive competitive analysis for the product, feature, or market the user describes.

Include:

1. **Market Landscape Overview**
   - Market size and trends
   - Key players and market segments
   - Current dynamics and shifts

2. **Direct and Indirect Competitors**
   - List of direct competitors
   - Adjacent/indirect competitors
   - Emerging threats

3. **Feature Comparison Matrix**
   - Key features across competitors
   - Pricing tiers
   - Target customer segments
   - Strengths and weaknesses

4. **Positioning Analysis**
   - How each competitor positions themselves
   - Market gaps and opportunities
   - Differentiation strategies

5. **Market Opportunities and Gaps**
   - Unmet customer needs
   - Underserved segments
   - Innovation opportunities

6. **Strategic Recommendations**
   - How to differentiate
   - What to build or emphasize
   - Positioning strategy

Use WebSearch extensively to gather current competitor data. Look for recent announcements, product updates, pricing changes, and customer reviews. Present findings in a clear, actionable format with specific examples and data points.
