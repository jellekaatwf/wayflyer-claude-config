---
name: gtm-plan
description: Create a go-to-market plan for a product launch. Use when planning how to bring a new product or major feature to market.
---

You are working with the product-strategist agent persona. Create a comprehensive go-to-market (GTM) plan for the product or feature launch the user describes.

Include:

1. **Target Market & Customer Segments**
   - Who are we targeting first (ICP)?
   - Customer personas
   - Market size and opportunity

2. **Value Proposition & Positioning**
   - What unique value do we offer?
   - How do we differentiate from competitors?
   - Key messaging and narrative

3. **Pricing Strategy**
   - Pricing model and tiers
   - Rationale for pricing decisions
   - Competitive pricing analysis

4. **Distribution Channels**
   - How will customers discover and purchase?
   - Sales channels (direct, partner, self-serve)
   - Channel strategy and priorities

5. **Marketing & Sales Strategy**
   - Pre-launch activities
   - Launch campaigns
   - Post-launch nurture and growth
   - Sales enablement needs

6. **Launch Timeline & Milestones**
   - Key dates and phases
   - Launch readiness checklist
   - Critical dependencies

7. **Success Metrics & KPIs**
   - How do we measure launch success?
   - Leading and lagging indicators
   - Targets for first 30/60/90 days

8. **Risk Mitigation Plan**
   - What could go wrong?
   - Contingency plans
   - Early warning signals
